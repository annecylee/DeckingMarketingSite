Why fiberglass Decking?

Fiberglass is a strong, lightweight material. This versatile substance doesn't rot, rust, or corrode, and it's resistant to both chemicals and impacts. Commonly used in boats and aircraft, Boltix has innovatively applied fiberglass to decking boards, creating a product that's exceptionally durable, weather-resistant, and maintenance-free while offering superior stability and longevity.
